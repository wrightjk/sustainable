Living More Sustainably

disclaimer: Some of the tips involved throughout this site for the purpose of transitioning to a more sustainable lifestyle are not feasible for everyone's life situation, and that's okay! Living more sustainably is not a one size fits all kind of idea. The primary focus is on the upkeep of the symbiotic relationship between humans and their environment, and every little contribution to that relationship is significant. If you can't do every action listed here, it's okay. Every step in the right direction is a significant step in the right direction.
